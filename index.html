About me

At age 7 I began fixing my family’s internet as my older sister always struggled with this task. From that point on I was declared the family’s technician and would work to solve any technological issue that arose. As time and technology advanced so did my passion for tech. This passion grew into a hobby that led me to build my own PC in middle school. My love for technology has led to my career path as a software engineer and encourages continuous learning in this expansive field. Being able to bring a sense of relief and happiness to my friends and family once I’ve resolved the technological issue has brought me joy through the years. I want to provide the same sense of relief and joy to my community through my work as a software engineer.
